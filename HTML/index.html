the restaurantA little story about us


Combining art and traditional asian cuisine and service to create a unique culinary experience in Lakewood Ranch as we at Hana Sushi Lounge strived for each day. With a fusion of artful sushi, fresh hand-picked produce, fire kitchen items, and hand crafted cocktails, there's always something for everyone to enjoy. No matter the occasion our friendly and knowledgeable staff will look forward to making your visit special every time.

- Owner, Jane Kim
